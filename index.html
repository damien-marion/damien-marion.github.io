Who Am I?

I am currently Postdoc in the EMSEC (Capsule) team, inside the IRISA laboratory. I am supervised by Annelie Heuser.

I am involved in two ANR:

  • AHMA: Automated Hardware Malware Analysis,
  • IDROMEL: Improving the Design of secure systems by a Reduction Of Micro-architectural Effects on side-channeL Attacks Images (IRISA, LAAS-CNRS, LIP6, CEA-list, ARM, DGA)

I was previously:

  • Postdoc in the MAIS team, inside TU-Darmstadt. I was supervised by Heiko Mantel, involved in the DFG collaborative research center CROSSING and created the side-channel lab.

I completed my PhD at Secure-IC and COMELEC supervised by Sylvain Guilley:

Multidimensionality of the models and the data in the side-channel domain. (Multidimensionnalité des modèles et des données dans le domaine des canaux auxiliaires)

Damien Marion - PhD thesis - Télécom Paris, France (2018)
