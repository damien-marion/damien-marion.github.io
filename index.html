Who Am I?

I am currently Postdoc in the EMSEC (Capsule) team, inside the IRISA laboratory. I am supervised by Annelie Heuser.

I am involved in:

  • the ANR AHMA: Automated Hardware Malware Analysis,
  • the ANR IDROMEL on the power side-channel leakages induced by micro-architectural design choices (with Arm France, CEA, IRISA, LAAS, Sorbonne University).

I was previously:

  • Postdoc in the MAIS team, inside TU-Darmstadt. I was supervised by Heiko Mantel, involved in the DFG collaborative research center CROSSING and created the side-channel lab.

I completed my PhD at Secure-IC and COMELEC supervised by Sylvain Guilley:
